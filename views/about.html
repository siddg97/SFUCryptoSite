About Us

The goal of the SFU Cryptocurrency Club is to raise awareness of Cryptocurrency, Blockchain, and distributed ledger technology (DLT). We provide networking opportunities with industry professionals, host info sessions and workshops.

We provide a platform for all SFU students to share and discuss all things Cryptocurrency. The topics that cover include but are not limited to DLT, technical analysis, market trends, and possible trading opportunities. We allow students at SFU engaged or interested in the Cryptocurrency space to bounce ideas off one another and learn from one another. Our main communication medium is our Discord Channel.
